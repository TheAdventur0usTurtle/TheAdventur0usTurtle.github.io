I have many hobbies, but my favorite has to be making things. More specifically, I like making dragons. And drawing dragons. Basically anything that involves dragons. You get the point. Oh, and before you ask: yes, I have read Wings of Fire and yes, I liked the series.

Actually, it was the cover art and the dragons in the book series that first inspired me. I learned how to draw dragons by copying them. Since then, I've moved on to drawing and creating more original things, but most of them are still dragons.

Anyways, here are just some of my favorite projects in no particular order. If you want to see what I'm doing right now, go to my Latest and Greatest page!

Drawings

I draw lots of dragons. Everywhere. Spare papers, margins, and notebook covers are all commonly vandalized by me. But out of all of them, these are my favorites.

Recently, I got some alcohol markers. However, my usual art style wouldn't work very well with them, so I tried something a little different and created some characters that used a new style. Overall, I think that they turned out pretty well.

I've also leared how to properly draw hands, heads, and human beings in general. It's the first time that I have freehand drawn people, but after learning the proportions, it was much easier than before.

Here are some heads. I'm too scared to try putting faces on them though, so they don't fall into the uncanny valley zone.
